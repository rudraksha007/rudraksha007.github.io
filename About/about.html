Introduction

“Nurturing minds , shaping futures.”

Sanskriti KMV School, CBSE affiliated, Sr. Sec. Co-Educational School, Jalandhar, is a renowned pioneer institution in the region, known for its deep-rooted motto of “Sanskriti Surbhit Srijan”. The school works on instilling ethos through comprehensive education to empower the young generation to thrive limitlessly.

Under the leadership of Mrs. Rachna Monga, Principal, Sanskriti KMV School has reached new heights, supported by the eminent Sh. Chander Mohan, President of Arya Shiksha Mandal, Prof. Dr. Mrs. Atima Sharma Dwivedi, Manager, and the visionary School Management.

School has an excellently built infrastructure designed for both online and on-campus teaching from Kindergarten to XII. The campus is equipped with Wi-Fi, and the classrooms are updated with smart teaching technology, providing Sanskriti KMV School with a competitive advantage amidst the challenges posed by COVID to the education sector. Additionally, the introduction of biometric attendance and Digital School ID cum Debit cards in 2023 has enhanced its technological features. The campus is under complete CCTV surveillance with over 100 HD cameras, and the school transport is GPS powered, ensuring efficient operations.

At our school, we believe in holistic education, extending beyond traditional classrooms. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities to cultivate new interests, hobbies, and skills in our students. Our focus is on delivering exceptional education, empowering students to become self-assured, autonomous, and accountable individuals prepared to face global challenges

With a plethora of accomplishments, it is a moment of joy to celebrate the school’s recognition by CBSE with its most prestigious programs. Sanskriti KMV School has created a School Innovation Cell in collaboration with AICTE and CBSE. School Innovation Council

School Innovation Council is an initiative launched by MIC in 2022, to enable the schools to bridge the gap in connecting schools with innovation and entrepreneurial enabler ecosystem of the country. Sanskriti KMV School crowns this privilege as a recognized Innovation Cell, to provide the platform for the school academia, industry experts, innovators and entrepreneurs to come together and work in unison to make continuous progress

In this process, Sanskriti KMV School implements and initiates the chain of programs supplemented with innovation related activities through the establishment of SIC Committee.

Sanskriti KMV School is the only school in Jalandhar and one of the 500 schools at national level, shortlisted by CBSE for the pilot project PASSPORT to EARNING(P2E) as e-learning activities by the Department of Skill Education CBSE , New Delhi.

Passport to earning (P2E) is an initiative that empowers students in the age group of 14 and above to be equipped with the relevant 21st Century skills to thrive in life providing them with future pathways to entrepreneurship, employment and social impact opportunities.

SKILL HUB INITITAIVE, PMKVY 2,O

Sanskriti KMV School in Collaboration with Skill Hub Initiative PMKVY feels fortunate to be chosen as the centre by CBSE to actualise the intent of NEP 2020 on the ground and to mainstream vocational education. Sanskriti KMV School stands privileged and honoured to be selected as the one of the centres to the in-school, drop outs and out of education candidates to design their own paths of study and life-plans. Under the Skill Hub Initiative (SHI),we aim to provide shared infrastructure, align with the needs of the local economy and address the needs of all target segments for vocational training.
