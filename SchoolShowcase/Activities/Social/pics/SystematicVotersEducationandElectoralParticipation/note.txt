“The vote is the most powerful nonviolent tool we have. – Use it wisely”Sanskriti KMV School gave an opportunity to all the students in grades 6 to 12 to actively participate in SVEEP (Systematic Voters’ Education and Electoral Participation) activities to understand the importance of right to vote. As responsible citizens of our nation, it is imperative that we understand the importance of our electoral system and actively engage in shaping our democracy. Voting is the cornerstone of democracy, serving as a fundamental right and civic duty in many societies.The students joined hands through a series of activities in this noble endeavour in order to make a meaningful impact on our society."
