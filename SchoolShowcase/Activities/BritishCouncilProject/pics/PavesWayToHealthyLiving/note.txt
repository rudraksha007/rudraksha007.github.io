Sanskriti KMV School under its International Dimensions in School, British Council Project, marked World Health Day 2022 with an extensively designed program to invoke awareness for good health and surroundings. Following the theme propogated by WHO for 2022 : Our Planet Our Health, students laid out an exhibit of the healthy food products and its sources. Foundation grades joined in to observe the exhibits and shop the food products of their choice as a part of their making a right choice and learning for healthy as well as environment friendly food products and habits. Mrs Rachna Monga, Principal in her special appreciation meet for the innovative program, shared her words with the students and praised their interest in identifying the healthy needs and shunning the junk food practice.
