Sanskriti KMV School students as a messengers of Global Harmony connected to the students of Fawwar Girls High School, Palestine, to share their strong concern for peace and humanity. Global online conversation made both the school students meet and converse with each other sharing cultural roots also. Sanskriti KMV School students exchanged their good wishes with the students of Palestine. Together they prayed for the safety of the children and people in the war traumatized Ukraine. Mrs Rachna Monga, Principal, in her elated appreciation comment over the global online exchange program, mentioned that children all over the world share common concern for education, growth and safety. Peace is the only way to the humanitarian progress, and not war.
