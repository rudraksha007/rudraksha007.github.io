Wisdom Wise School




School Timings

  • Students who utilize the school transport services must ensure they are at their designated pick-up points at least 10 minutes before the scheduled pick-up time
  • Students who travel to school using their own means of transportation, should plan their journey to ensure they arrive at school at least 15 minutes before the start of the first class.
  • Punctuality is a shared responsibility between students and parents. Parents are encouraged to support their children in developing good time management habits

Parental Counsel

  • Parents play a vital role in helping students develop good habits that contribute to their overall well-being and success.
  • Encouraging diligence in students is a shared responsibility. Parents can support their children by providing a conducive environment for studying.
  • Parents are encouraged to actively participate in school events, parent-teacher meetings, and other activities that foster a strong school community.

Admissions

  • Students must carry their identity card, which includes the latest photograph of the student in their school.
  • The school diary is a vital tool for communication between the school and parents. It contains important information like the timetable, homeworks and notices from the school
  • It is the responsibility of both students and parents to ensure that these items are brought to school daily as the student will not be allowed to attend the school without these
